## 🌟 Featured Project: iFoundU

### [iFoundU - Find Your Missing Pair](https://khaneliyas01.github.io/ifounduv1/)

A revolutionary marketplace application that connects people who have lost one item from a pair with those who have found or are selling matching pieces.

**Key Features:**
- 🛒 **Buy & Sell**: Post and purchase missing pairs (earbuds, shoes, gloves, jewelry, socks, rings, and more)
- 🔍 **Smart Categories**: Filter by product type and condition (New, Like New, Good, Fair)
- 📍 **Location-based**: Find items near you
- 👤 **User Accounts**: Manage your listings and purchases
- 📱 **Responsive Design**: Works seamlessly on all devices

**Tech Stack:**
- Frontend: HTML, CSS, JavaScript
- Deployment: GitHub Pages

**Why iFoundU?**
We've all lost one earring, one glove, or one shoe. Instead of throwing away the remaining pair, iFoundU creates a sustainable solution by helping people complete their sets while reducing waste.
